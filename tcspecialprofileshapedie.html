Product Description:

Tungsten Carbide Special Profile Shape Die

We manufacture KWD Brand Tungsten Carbide Special Profile Shaped Dies, including dies with grooves, corners with specific radii, half corners, and other custom design profiles as per customer requirements.

Designed to suit various materials such as stainless steel, mild steel, EN series, and alloy steel, our dies are crafted using specific grades of TC pellets. With a precise and accurate profile, our dies ensure impeccable performance and durability.

Our dies are manufactured according to precise customer specifications to meet the technical requirements for wire/bar drawing. This includes selecting the appropriate pellet size, using the right carbide grades, and maintaining an accurate die profile for optimal performance.

Size Range: 10mm to 80mm.
