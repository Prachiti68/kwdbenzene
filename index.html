About Us

Crafting Precision, Building Trust Since 1965

Established in January 1965, Khurana Wire Drawing Dies Pvt. Ltd. is a leading manufacturer and exporter of Tungsten Carbide Drawing Dies, PCD Wire Drawing Dies, and related products. We offer a wide range of high-quality dies catering to industries like stainless steel, high-carbon wire, low-carbon wire, bead wire, mild steel, and aluminum alloy manufacturing.

As an ISO 9001:2015 Certified Company, we take pride in our superior craftsmanship, precision engineering, and commitment to quality. Our products are globally recognized for their durability, strength, and performance, making us a trusted name in the wire drawing industry.

Legacy of
Late Avsharan Singh Khurana

A Visionary Leader, A Timeless Inspiration

Our journey is deeply rooted in the vision and dedication of (Late) Shri Avsharan Singh Khurana (1940–2021). A pioneer in the wire and die industry, he was widely respected for his expertise and contributions.

Starting as a die maker in the 1950s, he went on to establish Bombay’s first unit for die manufacturing in 1965. His relentless pursuit of excellence and unwavering commitment to quality laid the foundation for what Khurana Wire Drawing Dies Pvt. Ltd. is today.

Under his leadership, our company flourished, introducing advanced die manufacturing technologies, expanding globally, and setting new benchmarks in the industry. Today, we honor his legacy by continuing his passion for innovation, precision, and customer satisfaction.
